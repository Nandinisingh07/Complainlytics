Service Level Agreement (SLA) & Escalation Matrix

Severity Classification and Timelines:
- Critical (e.g., Active fraud, system outage): 4 hours
- High (e.g., Blocked card, urgent medical loan): 24 hours
- Medium (e.g., General complaints, fee reversals): 72 hours
- Low (e.g., Information request, branch feedback): 168 hours (7 days)

Escalation Mapping:
- ATM / Debit Card -> ATM & Card Operations Team
- Credit Card -> Credit Card Disputes Team
- Fund Transfer / NEFT / RTGS -> NEFT/RTGS Operations Team
- Internet Banking -> Digital Banking Support
- Mobile Banking App -> Mobile Banking Dev Team
- KYC / Documentation -> KYC Compliance Team
- Loan Services -> Loan Operations Department
- Account Opening -> Account Services Team
- Fixed Deposit -> Deposits & Treasury Team
- Customer Service -> Branch Service Quality Team

Escalation Protocol:
1. If SLA is breached, escalate immediately to the Level 1 manager of the mapped department.
2. If SLA breach exceeds 48 hours, escalate to the Zonal Head.
3. If SLA breach exceeds 5 days, escalate to Principal Nodal Officer and trigger a regulatory compliance alert.
